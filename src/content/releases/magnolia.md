I began recording 'Magnolia' in the weeks after my sister passed away. I felt overwhelmed with a kind of nostalgia, and when I began to explore that, this album took form. These songs play on themes of memory, and the passage of time; concepts that define our lives and help us to navigate our lives in past and present, in reflections, in parallel with ourselves.
