Inspired by light and sensory perception, psychedelics, religious imagery, and cyclical patterns.

These recordings were made by processing and arranging sampled material from records, field recordings, archival audio, synthesizers, and acoustic instruments (autoharp, cymbals, wind chimes). The majority of processing was done on a modular synthesizer with some additional treatments, arrangement, and mixing on the computer.
