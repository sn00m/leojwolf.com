About

Leo Wolf is an artist and engineer. His music combines synthesis with processed samples, field recordings, and archival sounds to create hypnotic drones and textures. He also explores visual art mediums such as monotype printmaking.

His work is heavily inspired by themes of memory and dreams. In both the audio and visual realms he investigates connections between the physical and metaphysical, the labyrinth of inner and outer realms.
