Leo Wolf is a musician and artist from Charlotte, North Carolina. While better known for his work in the drone/ambient genres, Leo got his start making psychedelic beat music under the alias Snoom, and developed a love for working with samples and heavily effected sounds. An exploratory instinct in his approach to music led Leo towards deeper concepts of production and sound design, involving modular analog gear to study various synthesis and sampling techniques.

Taking inspiration from symbolism in dreams, reflections, memory, and time, Leo seeks to form and refine connections between the physical and metaphysical nature of existence. The labyrinth represents that concept of how the inner parallels the outer.
