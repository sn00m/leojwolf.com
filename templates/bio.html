Artist Statement

My name is Leo Wolf, I’m an artist from North Carolina. For the last decade I’ve mainly focused on music, performing live, as a solo act and in collaboration with other artists, at venues and music festivals. I’ve also recorded five albums in that time, and worked as a sound designer for audiovisual installations. My music combines synthesis with processed samples, field recordings, and archival sounds to create hypnotic drones and textures. I also explore visual art mediums such as monotype printmaking.

My work is heavily inspired by themes of memory and dreams. In both audio and visual mediums I investigate connections between the physical and metaphysical, the labyrinth of inner and outer realms, seeking an understanding of the forms we take, and how they define our lives.
